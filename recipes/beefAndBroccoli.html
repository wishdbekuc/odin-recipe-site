Beef & Broccoli stir-fry Recipe

back

The classic chinese cuisine, beef & broccoli stir-fry is a fast and delightfully savoury dish that is a household name in most asian families.

Ingredients

    Beef marinade

  • 300g beef slices
  • 2 tsp ShaoXing Wine
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp seasame oil
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

    • Sauce

  • 1 tbsp dark soy
  • 1 tbsp light soy
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar

    • Broccoli

  • 300g Broccoli (chopped into bite sized pieces)

    • Stir-fry

  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 slice ginger (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ShaoXing Wine
  • 2 tsp cornstarch

Directions

  1. Mix beef and marinade. Set aside.
  2. Combine sauce ingredients in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Bring salted water up to a boil. Add broccoli and cook for 1.5 minute. Remove from water and set aside to air dry.
  4. Heat pan over high heat. Add touch of oil and sear beef. Ensure that beef does not overcrowd the pan as it will steam instead.
  5. Stir the beef. Add minced garlic and ginger. Fry until fragrant.
  6. Add sauce and stir well. Add broccoli and toss contents until covered in sauce.
  7. Mix 2 tbsp cornstarch with cool water. Add mixture slowly to pan to thicken sauce. (add only until sauce reaches desired thickness)
  8. Serve!
